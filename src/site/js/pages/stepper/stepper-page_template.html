# Stepper The stepper's dots can be hovered, displaying a tooltip with the text that was given to that dot's definition object. The dots can also be clicked to mark them as the currently active step. Only one step can be active at the time. A step that has its `isEnabled` property in its object definition won't be shown in the stepper. **Important: Steps can't have duplicated ids.** Otherwise, an error will be logged and will lead to **unexpected results.** ## Demo
