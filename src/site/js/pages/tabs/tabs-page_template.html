### Generating tabs dynamically The tabs may also be dynamically generated, by using the `v-for` directive on a `wc-tab` element. In mobile, the tabs' width will be the same. Only four tabs will be displayed at the time and if there are more than four, the tabs will be scrollable and on tab the scroll position will change to fully visualize the selected option.
